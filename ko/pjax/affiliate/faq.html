일반

What is the Binary.com Affiliate Programme all about?

The Binary.com Affiliate Programme allows you to benefit from referring new clients to our site.

If you're a broker we'd like to work with you. You will be introducing your clients to a unique and innovative product: the Binary.com trading platform. Your clients will love the Binary.com trading platform because we offer a complete binary options trading experience tailored to the needs of an exceptionally wide range of traders.

Novice traders can use our intuitive platform to learn about trading, practise trading, and gain trading experiences. Seasoned traders can use the Binary.com platform and take full advantage of the wide range of trading and analysis tools we have to offer.

Why should I become a Binary.com affiliate?

Binary.com is a licensed and regulated binary options trading platform that’s been operating since 2000. It has:

  • An international appeal with multilingual support in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Italian, Thai, Polish, Russian, and Indonesian
  • An intuitive, web-based platform that’s instantly available to traders of all levels – anytime, anywhere
  • A competitive and flexible affiliate programme that can be adapted to your needs

가입에 필요한 비용이 있습니까?

Not at all. Joining our affiliate programme is completely free and always will be.

What is the definition of a referred client?

A client is someone who has been referred through your unique affiliate link and who has deposited money into their Binary.com account. They must fulfil the following criteria:

  • Have not previously been a Binary.com customer
  • Aged 18 years old and above

누가 Binary.com 플랫폼의 고객이 될 수 있나요?

Anyone aged 18 years old and above who is not the resident of a “restricted country” (as listed in our Terms & Conditions) can become a Binary.com client.

It’s easy. Just go to the signup page and complete the affiliate registration form. We’ll review your application and get back to you if your application is successful.

How can I change my payment method?

Log in to your Binary.com affiliate account and go to: Finances → Payment instructions.

제 제휴 소득을 어떻게 그리고 언제 받게 되나요?

Your commission for the previous calendar month will be deposited into your account by the 15th of every month.

저의 수익을 아려면 어떻게 해야 하나요?

Log in to your Binary.com affiliate account and go to: Reports → Detailed activity report

What kind of reports can I generate?

You can generate all kinds of insightful reports, including the following:

  • Hits & Impression report: Displays your hit rate and click through rates
  • Countries report: Displays a list of countries where your clicks are coming from
  • My Players report: Displays a list of your clients according to their IDs and the date they signed up

Can I offer my referrals an incentive to sign up through my unique affiliate link rather than sign up directly?

We prohibit the use of unauthorised incentives, gifts, and payments to encourage customer signups. If we have reason to believe that you have engaged in such activity, we reserve the right to withhold any payments due to you. However, if you have a specific incentive in mind, then please contact your account manager for further discussion and approval.

마케팅 및 홍보

어떤 광고 자료와 마케팅 도구들을 제공하나요?

저희는 귀하께서 이용하실 수 있도록 배너, 리뷰, 비디오, 그리고 텍스트 광고를 포함한 시험을 거치고 검증된 광고 자료를 가지고 있습니다. 만약 특정 항목을 귀하께서 선호하는 방식으로 맞추고 싶다면, affiliates@binary.com의 저희 계정 관리자에게 연락주세요. 귀하께서는 또한 Myaffiliates 시스템을 사용하여 귀하의 캠페인을 추적하고 최적화할 수 있습니다.

광고 자료들이 어디에 위치하고 있나요?

귀하의 Binary.com 제휴 계정에 로그인하셔서 마케팅 → 모든 미디어에 접근하세요.

네 그렇습니다. 다음의 정보와 함께 제휴 관리자에게 연락해주시기 바랍니다:

  • 픽셀 사이즈
  • 파일 형식
  • 희망 언어
  • 배너가 표시될 사이트의 URL
  • 배너 게재위치
  • 배너가 포함 해야하는 특정 색상
  • 배너가 포함 해야하는 특정 문구
